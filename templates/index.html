What To Know


How does this work?

You fill out the application, we handle the rest. If you meet our qualifications, we'll introduce you to our hiring partners. We receive a lot of applications, so it might be 7-10 days before we get back to you.

What type of candidates are you looking for?

We work with a variety of talented candidates, spanning the typical roles associated with tech companies. This includes engineering (software developers, data scientists, etc), product management (including analysts), sales and marketing, and design. If you do not fit into one of these disciplines, but still consider yourself a strong potential employee, contact us at kendall@hawkhire.com

What kind of companies do you work with?

We work mainly with tech companies, though a few of our partner companies span multiple industries. We look for high-growth companies with unique, challenging hiring needs, and strive to make the process as efficient and painless as possible.

Will you contact my current company?

No. We understand it is not always in your best interest to let your company know you are looking for a new job. Rest assured, we will not contact any companies listed on your resume without your express consent.
