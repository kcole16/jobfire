Frequently Asked Questions


How does this work?

We help you find a job in two ways. You can use our search and research tools to find jobs you like, then apply to or follow them. Additionally, we'll discover and send you jobs we think will interest you, and contact the companies on you behalf, if you'd like.

Why are some jobs “follow” and others “apply”?

Positions that are "followable" aren't open just yet, but will be soon. These companies want to know which students are interested when they are ready to hire. Positions marked with "Apply" are actively looking for candidates.

What happens when I click "follow"?

By following a position, your are indicating your interest. We will send your resume and profile to the hiring manager, and they will notify you when the position is open. Additionally, they may contact you in advance with different opportunities.

What happens when I click "apply"?

Clicking apply indicates your immediate availability and interest in the position. Your information will be sent to the hiring manager, and if there’s a fit, they will reach out to you directly.

What type of companies do you work with?

We work exclusively with high-growth, venture-backed companies. These include post-Series A to later stage startups, and exceptional pre-Series A companies. We do not work with recruiting agencies.

How long will it take to hear back?

For applications, you should hear something within two - three weeks. Follows operate differently, and some companies send updates more frequently than others. You will be notified when the job opens, and might receive other news regarding networking events.
